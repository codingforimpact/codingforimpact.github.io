Our Roots

NYC Connector

NYC Connector commenced the work we do at Coding for Impact: driving positive change. In the summer of 2018, our co-founder William was having trouble finding places to volunteer near him — that's when the spark for NYC Connector was born: an application that connects the thousands of unknown charities and nonprofits with the millions of children and adults looking to give back to their community in NYC.

E-commerce

Paw Prints in the Sand

Paw Prints in the Sand is a 501(c)(3) all animal rescue organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abused, abandoned, neglected, and stray companion animals. CEO Kelly Reeves asked us to develop an e-commerce platform through which users could buy and send e-cards to others, under the name and sponsorship of Paw Prints in the Sand.

Website Design

Resource Equality International

Resource Equality International (REI) has a mission to provide sustainable access to clean water and sanitation in developing countries. CEO Peter Williams asked us to revamp his site to better portray REI's message and mission to the world.

Backend Development

Democracy Without Borders

Democracy Without Borders strives for a democratic world order in which citizens participate beyond national boundaries in shaping policy that serves their joint long-term interests. Executive Director and acclaimed activism author Andreas Bummel asked us to work on backend development and resolve issues with the security of their website system.

Web Development

The Richard Brodsky Foundation

Richard M. Brodksy, after surviving HIV and terminal brain cancer, devoted the rest of his life to helping those who have been afflicted with HIV and brain cancer. The mission of his organization is "to provide food, shelter and shoes for orphans living in Kenya, raise money for research for the cure or vaccine for AIDS, to help those already afflicted with AIDS, and to help those with brain cancer." Richard's story is a true inspiration for us, and we were honored to be able to assist on the technical side with his website. We've been regularly updating his website with the most up to date relevant information, as well as providing content changes for his World Aids Marathon site.
