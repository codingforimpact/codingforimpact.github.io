Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver technology to underserved nonprofits and charities. Through technology, we aim to drive a positive and significant impact in our society.

Our Clients

Project Focus

NYC Connector

NYC Connector (nycconnector.org) was built with a mission in mind: to provide seamless access to the thousands of charities and nonprofits for the thousands in NYC looking to give back to their community. Our technology incorporates the most comprehensive database of organizations located in NYC, utilizing a simple interface to connect you with organizations. Our work was featured on NYC Open Data's Project Gallery: click here to check it out.

Our Recent Projects

E-commerce

Paw Prints in the Sand

Paw Prints in the Sand is a 501(c)(3) all animal rescue organization dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming abused, abandoned, neglected, and stray companion animals. CEO Kelly Reeves asked us to develop an e-commerce platform through which users could buy and send e-cards to others, under the name and sponsorship of Paw Prints in the Sand.

Website Design

Resource Equality International

Resource Equality International (REI) has a mission to provide sustainable access to clean water and sanitation in developing countries. CEO Peter Williams asked us to revamp his site to better portray REI's message and mission to the world.

Backend Development

Democracy Without Borders

Democracy Without Borders strives for a democratic world order in which citizens participate beyond national boundaries in shaping policy that serves their joint long-term interests. Executive Director and acclaimed activism author Andreas Bummel asked us to work on backend development and resolve issues with the security of their website system.
