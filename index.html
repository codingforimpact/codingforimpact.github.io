Coding for Impact


Founded in the Summer of 2018, Coding for Impact is a student-led club formed at Hunter College High School, dedicated to driving a positive impact in our society, through the implementation of software-based services for non-profit organizations, charities, and education platforms.





About Us


We are a group of highly motivated and hard-working high school students, willing to work with any non-profit on any type of project, from web design to app development to even search engine optimization.


Projects


During and outside of school, we actively work on volunteer projects for non-profits, charities, and causes that we care about. We deployed our first project, NYC Connector, in the summer of 2018. NYC Connector allows users to easily find and locate nearby places where they can volunteer at, donate to, or find help at in NYC.

Mission


Our mission is to contribute to the community as much as we can by applying our passions of coding to help non-profits, charities, and community service centers. Our goal is to have a positive impact on the world through code.
