Coding for Impact


We're a group of high school students in NYC who help build websites and mobile applications for non-profit organizations and charities.



About Us


Founded in the Summer of 2018, Coding for Impact is an organization of high school students based in NYC, dedicated to driving a positive impact in our society through the implementation of software-based services for non-profit organizations, charities, and education platforms. We use our skills in technology to help other non-profits further their good causes, and also build apps of our own to help the community.

We are a group of highly motivated and hard-working high school students, willing to work with any non-profit on any type of project, from web design to app development to even search engine optimization.


Mission


Our mission is to further social good in our community through our work with non-profits and charities, as well as through independent projects to positively impact society. We strive to empower non-profits by helping them develop applications which allow them to promote their causes and connect with their audiences.


Projects


During and outside of school, we actively work on projects for non-profits, charities, and causes that we care about. We deployed our first project, NYC Connector, in the summer of 2018. NYC Connector allows users to easily find and locate nearby places where they can volunteer at, donate to, or find help at in NYC. Click here to see the code.


We have partnered with a number of unique organizations to provide our services in web and app development. Some organizations we're working with right now are Tutoria, The Daily Source, as well as Growing Green Growing People.
