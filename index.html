TECHNOLOGICAL APPROACH

A THOUGHTFUL AND INTEGRATIVE APPROACH THAT DEFINES US





THINK


Every problem has a solution. The first hurdle? Identifying that problem. Our Project and Vision Coordinators work toward recognizing specific problems in our society. DESIGN + BUILD


The execution of a product is what makes it a viable and desired tool for people to use. When working on projects or with clients, our designers collaborate to (1) understand the vision and mission of the project, and (2) develop a layout and optimizing the experience of the intended user. Our developers design a few prototypes of the project, highlighting different techniques, and we then collaborate on a final design driven by the objective of the project.



PUBLISH


Once we have completed the development stages, we move on to publish the project online for everyone to see.

WHY US?





Fast Turnaround

We are quick and motivated to tackle any projects we undertake, and dedicate our time to completing it in a timely manner. We're fast workers, and will immediately start working on your project once we receive it. NYC Connector was completed in less than 3 days!



Expertise Coding Skills

OUR WORK

CURRENT AND COMPLETED PROJECTS



NYC CONNECTOR (COMPLETED)

Built in the summer of 2018, NYC Connector is a project which allows users to easily find and locate places near them where they can volunteer at, donate to, or find help at, such as soup kitchens, food pantries, SNAP centers, senior centers, food scrap drop-off sites, as well as clothing charities, and homeless shelters. Enter an address or zip code and select the places you want to see, and resources nearby will be displayed on the map. Click on a pinpoint marker to view information about the resource. It's as easy as that!
