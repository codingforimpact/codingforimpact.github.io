Big Goals, Significant Change

Coding for Impact (CFI) begins here: our vision is to use technology to eliminate the socioeconomic barriers hindering nonprofits from reaching their full potential. Our work is divided into two categories: (1) developing independent projects to address issues in our community and (2) developing websites and software for nonprofits and charities in need of technical assistance and a greater web presence. Our philosophy revolves around readily perceiving social and humanitiarian issues in our community and devising software-based solutions to address them. In developing websites for nonprofits and charities, we strive first to understand their mission and purpose to create websites that best showcase their values. We collaborate closely with clients to provide the most impactful visual experience.
