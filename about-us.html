ABOUT US

A Coding Organization Dedicated to Making an Impact Through Technology

Formed at Hunter College High School, we're a group of talented high school programmers dedicated to using our skills in technology to empower underserved and underesourced nonprofits and charities in need of technical assistance. Moreover, we work to build solutions to solve social and humanitarian issues in our community.
